Chocolate Banana Peanut Butter Protein Shake

This chocolate protein shale is high in protein and tasty

Description

A simple recipe for a chocolate Banana peanut butter protein shake that is high in protein and tasty. Prep time is less than 10 minutes and serves 1.

Ingredients

  • 6 ice cubes
  • 1 cup of milk
  • 1 banana
  • 1 scoop of chocolate-flavoured protein powder
  • 2 tbps of peanut butter

Optional: 1 tbps of honey

Optional: 1 tbps of unsweetened coca powder

Directions

  1. Gather all ingredients and add into a blender.
  2. Blend all the ingredients in the blender until smooth. Usually less than a minute.

Serve

Pour into cup, grab a straw and enjoy!
