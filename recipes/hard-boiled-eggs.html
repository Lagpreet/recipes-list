Perfect Hard-Boiled Eggs

Use this recipe for perfectly cooked hard-boiled eggs. By adding a little vinegar and salt, the eggshell peels off so easily without tearing or sticking.

Description

This is a detailed step-by-step recipe for perfect hard-boiled eggs where the eggshells peel off easily!

Ingredients

  • Large Eggs
  • 6 cups of water
  • 1/4 cup of distilled white vinegar
  • 1 tbps of salt

Directions

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Combine salt, vinegar and water in a pot, and bring it to a boil over high heat.
  3. Add eggs one at a time, reduce the heat to a gentle boil and cook for 14 minutes.
  4. Once cooked, remove them from the hot water and place into a container of ice or cold water, can even be another pot of running cold water from the tap. Cool completely for 15 minutes.

Serve

Eggs should be easy to peel. Enjoy!
