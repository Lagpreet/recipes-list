Butter Chicken Recipe

This butter chicken recipe by Chef Varun Inamdar is simple yet heavenly variation on the cuisine

Description

A simple yet tasty recipe for the most popular indian cuisine the Butter Chicken. Serves 4-5 people.

Ingredients

For the chicken

  • boneless chicken breast
  • 1 tbsp Ginger garlic paste
  • 1 tbsp Red chilli powder
  • 1 tbsp salt

For the gravy

  • chopped tomatoes
  • chopped onions
  • cashews
  • 1 tbsp garlic paste
  • 1 tbsp kasoori methi
  • 1/2 tbsp garam masala
  • 4 tbsp sugar
  • 2 tbsp kashmiri chilli powder
  • 5 tbsp butter
  • 3 tbsp cream
  • 2 tbsp malt vinegar or 1 tbsp White Vinegar
  • 1 tbsp salt

Directions

  1. Mix and Marinate the chicken with all of the ingredients for it, keep it aside for 15 to 20 minutes.
  2. In a heated pan, add oil and fry the chicken.
  3. In the same pan, add butter, tomatoes, onions and cashews.
  4. Add water, garlic paste, salt, malt vinegar, sugar, garam masala powder and chilli powder, mix everything and let it simmer for 15-20 minutes.
  5. Churn the mixture into a fine puree using a hand blender.
  6. Strain the puree back into the same pan.
  7. Add Butter, cream, chicken and kasoori methi and let it simmer for 5-7 minutes.

Serve

Now you can starting plating the dish. Garnish it with cream and and a sprinkle of kasoori meethi. Serve with rice or naan and enjoy!
